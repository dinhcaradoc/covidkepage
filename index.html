Corona Virus is a new respiratory disease that attacks the lungs. It is highly contagious and deadly. Individuals are advised to closely adhere to the recommended preventive measures.

Government Advisory: Avoid Covid-19. Stay Safe. Stay At Home.

What is Covid-19?

Covid-19 is a respiratory illness affecting the lungs. It is caused by a new virus, the novel corona virus. The disease is easily spread through contact with droplets from an infected person when this person coughs or sneezes, or sometimes just when they talk. Transmission can also occur through contact with surfaces and objects that have been contaminated. No treatment or vaccine is available for the disease, since it is viral-based. LEARN MORE

What are the symptoms of Covid-19?

Since it is a flu, patients sick with Covid-19 exhibit similar symptoms. Nonetheless, there are several key differences. The Covid-specific symptoms are as follows:

  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Tiredness (Fatigue)
  • Shortness of breath

How to Prevent Covid-19

There are number of measures that you can take to stop Covid-19. These measures target a reduction in transmission to stave off the virus. These measures include:

  • Wash your hands using soap and running water.
  • Clean your hands using an alcohol-based sanitizer.
  • Keep a personal space of at least 2 meters.
  • Avoid close personal contact (hugging, shaking hands, kissing) with people with flu-like symptoms.
  • Stay at home and avoid non-essential travel
